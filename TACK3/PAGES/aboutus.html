Know us

This gym was created by a couple who went out to train a lot.Our goal is to pass on the acquired knowledge.After traveling the world and training in different places we decided to study more professionally to teach and help.Today we are adding more people so that we can train together daily, and they can also have access to the necessary elements to exercise.
